Chapter 1: Disappearance of Mr. Wright


Previously: You successfully joined the Agil's Cryptography division for summer internship.


Next day, you get your first assignment letter.

You are very excited but also become nervous as you read the contents.


A G I L    I n v e s t i g a t i o n     I n c .
Spellborough, U.K.

4 May, 2009

Assignment #1

OBJECTIVE: Decipher the message.

DESCRIPTION: Our client Mrs. Tamsyn Wright has reported her husband missing from longer than a week. She has a letter that he has written to her, but the contents of that letter are cryptic to her senses, as they are encrypted using Caesar Cipher. We have found the related information necessary to decrypt that code, and have mentioned that below. Your task is to submit to us the original message after decryption.

CIPHER INFO: Caesar Cipher. Key: 13.

Encl. (2)
  1. Partial Statement report of Mrs. Wright.
  2. Letter from Mr. Wright to his wife.


CLIENT STATEMENT REPORT
Client Name: Mrs. Tamsyn Wright
Client Address: 5B, 10th Street, Starrybourne
Date: 3 May, 2009

...
"On 21st April, my husband told me that he had to go out urgently on an interstate meeting. (He is a freelance cryptographer and provides consultancy to clients.) At that time, he assured me of his return in three days, but it has been more than a week, and there is no sign of him except a letter that he sent on 22nd.
Partial contents of his letter are possibly encrypted, and make no sense to me."
...

22 April, 2009
Dear Tamsyn,
Yesterday, I had to hastily leave out of town due to a surprise meeting, and so, was unable to describe to you the details of this trip. I am very sorry for that. Regretfully, I cannot share those details even now, as the gravity of the matters surrounding this trip have dramatically increased today.
Don't worry though, as I will surely return back before our anniversary on 26th, and we'll celebrate at the Laurel's.
Take care.

With love.
Yours forever,
Thomas Wright
