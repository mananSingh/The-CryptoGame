Previously: The Police and the investigators raided the COATIS headquarter and found huge evidence of their cyber crime activities. An official arrest warrant have been issued for everyone involved. Is everything solved now? Is Mr. Wright found or arrested?


You feel proud as you have just cracked a password hash. But you noticed something strange!

The password "Warthog_Smith" is an anagram of "Thomas_Wright". Can this mean something? Does some mystery remain unravelled even now?

The next day, early morning, you get another assignment - an encrypted report.

Anyway, since, an arrest warrant has been issued for him as well, we sent one of our investigator yesterday to his home for another survey and his capture, if found.
