Chapter 10: Interception


Previously: The investigations of disappearance of Mr. Wright led to a shadowy organization called COATIS. While the investigations regarding the case continued, you witnessed a strange case in which a client's digital files were encrypted automatically with a strong cryptographic mechanism; and the client was asked for a huge amount of money to decrypt her files.

You never thought that cryptography could be harmful to anyone, and here it is - an unusual case!

Who could have encrypted those files? Who owns the secret RSA private key which the previous report talked about?

And, what about the investigation regarding COATIS? What did they made out of the IP address that you decrypted?

After few days of wait, your next assignment comes. Possibly, A big revelation awaits ahead.
